About Us

Who Are We?

We're John, Ariana and Flynn the dog! We're from Salt Lake City, UT and bonded over our LOVE for food. The first time we camped together, John swore he'd never camp with me again, but a year later we were building our first offroad trailer with a rooftop tent setup. We've come to know the best camp recipes and have found amazing trails to offroad and experience a different side of Utah. We lead guided weekend warrior expeditions between the months of April to September to give you a taste of the overland life!

What to Expect

We leave at 8AM Thursday mornings to head to the destination you've selected. Once at the trail head we will stop for a quick briefing on safety and off-road best practices before hitting the dirt. We will provide all meals, if you have any dietary restrictions we're happy to accomodate or we can adjust your rate to bring your own food.

How to Apply for an Expedition

We have already opened our expeditions for 2021! Please contact us using the "Contact Us" page to request our schedule and availability. Our rates are dependent on the length of the trip, the vehicles being requested and the size of the group. We will get back to you with a quote within 24 ours of your expedition choice before requiring the down payment. Down payments are 25% of the expedition and are subject to change based on package choice.
